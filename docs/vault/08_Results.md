## Key Findings
1. **Anomaly detected** — the observed R_min distribution differs from LCDM at AD sig < 0.001 (combined sample)
2. **Tail excess** — 5/15 observed systems have R_min > 0.2 vs 4.2% expected (T = 8x)
3. **Optical drives the signal** — optical quads show stronger anomalies (possibly due to microlensing)
4. **Radio marginal** — 2/7 radio systems anomalous, AD sig = 0.078 (borderline)
5. **f_sub unconstrained** — the R_fold/R_cusp statistics cannot distinguish between f_sub values
